Instructions

Welcome to the final structural design project. This assignment requires you to synthesize the core principles of load-bearing analysis and material science covered in Modules 4 through 7.

Project Goal: Design a sustainable pedestrian bridge that spans 25 meters with a maximum deflection of no more than 0.05 meters under peak load.

  • Submit a detailed PDF containing CAD drawings.
  • Provide a material cost estimation spreadsheet.
  • Include a 500-word justification for your choice of truss structure.
